7. Architectural Overview

We utilize a Layered Micro-Kernel Architecture combined with a Client-Server model.

Rationale: We chose this to separate front-end concerns (Next.js) from the high-performance computation layer (FastAPI) while allowing for horizontal scaling of AI workers.
